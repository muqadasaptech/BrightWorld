Our Vision

Shanghai Wellmax Lighting Industry is a leading lighting company that focuses on the innovation and manufacture of LED bulbs. We were founded in 1987, and now a top exporter of LED bulbs in Shanghai. We are the LED Bulb Expert with over 37 years of global lighting experience. We are committed to providing the highest quality products through our strict quality assurance system and strong R&D capabilities. WELLMAX is the first company in China to become strategic partner with SAMSUNG.
